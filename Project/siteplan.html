Site Plan

Site

Discover Accra

This name reflects the site's purpose of exploring and showcasing the vibrant culture, history, and attractions of Accra, Ghana.

Optional Domain Availability: discover-accra.com

Site Purpose

The purpose of the site is to provide a comprehensive guide to Accra for both locals and tourists, featuring:

  • Historical and cultural landmarks.
  • Local events and festivals.
  • Recommendations for food, accommodations, and activities.
  • Practical travel tips and contact information.

Scenarios

  • Where can I find information about cultural landmarks in Accra?
  • What local festivals or events can I attend in Accra during my visit?

Color Schema

  • Primary Color: Deep Gold (#DAA520) - Used for headings, navigation bar, and accents.
  • Secondary Color: Deep Green (#006400) - Used for backgrounds and section dividers.
  • Neutral Color: Light Gray (#F5F5F5) - Used for page backgrounds.

Typography

  • Heading Font: Playfair Display - Used for major headings.
  • Body Font: Roboto - Used for paragraphs and general content.
  • Special Font: Lobster - Used for logo and call-to-action sections.
